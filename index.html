DOTS! FAQ

Q: What is a Dot?
A: Dots are small devices that help you save money and accomplish mundane daily tasks quickly and efficiently. They have been designed for you with two goals in mind. First, to have a tangible affect on your life. Second, to be extremely simple to use.
Q: How does a Dot work?
A: Dots work by connecting to the internet to pass information to and from other people, services and companies. Naturally, you get to choose what your dots do and who they communicate with. You do so through a one-time Dot setup on DotsHub.io, our online control platform for Dots owners.
